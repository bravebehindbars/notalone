No Excuse for Domestic Abuse

*DOMESTIC VIOLENCE RESOURCES FOR NEW ENGLAND*

1 in 3 women and 1 in 4 men report having experienced some form of domestic violence in their lifetime

You don't have to suffer alone. There is help. There are many resources to help keep you safe.

MISSION STATEMENT

Committed to offering resources to help women and men in New England safely escape their abusers. Breaking the silence and spreading awareness about Domestic Violence to reach those in need. Always aiming to lift others up and let you know that you are not alone. There is help, hope, and healing that you have a right to. Please reach out for help and please be safe. You are worthy of so much more that life has to offer!
